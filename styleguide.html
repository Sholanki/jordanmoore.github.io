Colours

Default black (#000), default White (#FFF) and default web link blue, usually (#00e)

A class of .dim is used occasionally for utility content which washes out the opacity of black by 60%.

Layout

Measurements inspired by the Golden Ratio for paragraph spacing. Optimal reading line-length considered. Other measurements are simply enough to maintain a decent information hierarchy. Just enough from defaults.
