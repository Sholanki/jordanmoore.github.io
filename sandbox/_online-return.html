Here’s everything you need to know about returning your item(s)

Conveniently return your item by mail following the instructions below.

Since you would like to return by mail:

  • Items must be returned, in new, unused condition, and in its original packaging, and with all paperwork including manuals, assembly instructions and your packing slip, as well as all accessories to ensure full credit.
  • Please remove any personal information or data from your item before sending.
  • If returning an item that was part of a larger Bundle, please return all associated items. We are unable to refund on individual items.

What to expect:

  • Online purchases returned within 90 days to our online Returns Center by mail and in “new” condition will receive a refund of the price of the item and any applicable taxes will be issued in the original form of payment used to purchase the item. See info below for items purchased with Pay in Store.
  • It normally takes 5-10 days for the returned item to arrive and be processed by the Returns Center.
  • Refunds are credited upon the item being processed and are generally available within 2 weeks of mailing your return. Please know that your bank may take several days to release the funds to your account.
  • Pay in Store items returned to the Online Returns Center will receive an eGift Card in the amount of the original purchase price. This eGift Card can be brought to any TRU/BRU store to have the amount refunded to the original form of payment.
  • Any product that is returned without a promotional item(s) that was included with the original transaction (e.g., buy a game console, get a free gift card) will have the value of the promotional item deducted from the refund amount.

Return fees:

  • A flat shipping fee determined by size, weight, and freight class will be deducted automatically from the item refund. Below are the label fees with the estimated package weight that qualifies for each one. The actual fee will be shown during the label creation process:
    Parcel Size Return Fee
    Small Parcel ( <5lbs.) .95
    Medium Parcel (>5lbs. and <70lbs.) .95
    Large items that were special delivered (>70lbs.) .95
  • Returns not meeting the return criteria may be returned to the customer without crediting a refund or may be issued a partial refund.
